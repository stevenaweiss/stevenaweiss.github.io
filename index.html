Portfolio

In 2014, after some time exploring on my own, I enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive with the goal of enhancing my programming abilities. I came away from the experience with a deep respect for code, and a hunger to learn more. I've learned that I have so much more to learn...which is quite exciting.

Below are a few projects I've worked on recently. They are, like everything else, works in progress. All can be viewed on my GitHub.

Jsequencr

A music sequencer, built mainly in Javascript (and jQuery) using the Rails framework. Designed out of a love for vintage drum machines, jSequencer follows the classic step sequencer pattern. Select some sounds, a tempo, and which steps to play...and suddenly all electronic, hip hop, and modern pop music is born. It's that simple. Also the drum sounds are an TR808 sampled through a cassette deck.

On The Side

I love unique side dishes and apps. I'd zipline over a lake of radioactive pirhanas for some delicious brussel sprouts. On The Side is a curated list of super trendy sides and apps searchable by neighborhood. Written in Ruby, via Rails, using the Foursquare, Instagram, and Google Static Maps API's.
